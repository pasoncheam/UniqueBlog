Rough Registraion


The experience that i’ll share here in our wonderful blog Unique Blog is how my registration in ucsi was not so easy, first of all i register in ucsi for mars-2021, but of course mister covid how destroyed the world oblige me to change my registration to sep-2021. But if this was the only problem that i had it’s be so easy, after that of course i need to pay my study fees and even if i got problem with my bank they assure me that the money will arrive to ucsi account in some days because of the international transfer, so i come to malaysia and i got hotel quarantine 14 days without going out of your room and alone i was going to be crazy, so after that hopefully i didn’t had a problem with the accommodation but miss life didn’t finish testing me, the day after i arrived in the accommodation i went to the visa office as a international student you need to register there for the student visa so when i went as an clumsy men i forgot my passport at home so i went back home and get it, so after that i needed to go to the north wing for the health check and i arrived at 1pm and it was lunchtime and the guard didn’t let me in so i can wait inside i waited for 1 hour outside the sun killed me (so sad), but hopefully the health check passed with just 1 problem that i’ll talk about it in another article where you’ll heard the rest of my story.
