Stories from Wong Min Jze


THE IRRESPONSIBLE BUS DRIVER


This happened on an ordinary day when I was just going to the UCSI campus as usual. I woke up early in the morning and started my daily routine. Currently, I will go to campus by bus and lrt since I don't have any other choices for transportation. After reaching the LRT station near the UCSI campus which has a shuttle bus service, I started to wait for the shuttle bus to arrive. According to the timetable that UCSI has given, the bus should have arrived around 7:30 a.m. However, when time has passed 7:30 a.m., the bus was still not there yet. I was thinking that the bus might just be a little late and I continued to wait. 5 minutes, 10 minutes, even 15 minutes has passed, the shutter bus was still not there yet. I was frustrated at the moment. Just then, another bus arrived, although it's not a shuttle bus, it's still a way to the UCSI campus. I got on the bus and thought I can finally get to campus in time. Just when I was thinking that I can arrive on campus in time, the bus driver stopped the bus beside a bus stop and he got off the bus. Looking at him getting off the bus, I thought the bus had any technical issue and he needed to get down the bus and have a look at it. I was wrong about it. In fact, the bus driver was just wanted to buy some drink. Just because he wanted to buy some drinks, he stopped the bus beside a bus stop. He was not only buying a drink, he even entered KK supermarket to walk around, just like window shopping. Eventually, I arrived at the campus, but I was already late for the classes. Until today, I am still remembering this incident. I will never forget such an irresponsible bus driver.
