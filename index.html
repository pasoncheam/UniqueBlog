WELCOME FELLOW USER!

Written and recorded by Pason Cheam Tung Huay, Feb 2, 2022


Greetings Internet peoples of the Internet, a warm welcome to UNIque Blog, a university blog-based website created by five foundation students from University College Sedaya International (UCSI). This website was created as the project for the web development course (FBF 1153). The name UNIque Blog was suggested by our team member, Wong Min Jze as it reflects our website's uniqueness along with being a university blog website, ergo the capital alphabets "UNI". This website will feature content such as personal stories and experiences from each of our team members in UCSI University, tips and pieces of advice on how to survive university life, and also a photo gallery of pictures around the UCSI Kuala Lumpur campus.

We hope you, our handsome and beautiful user will enjoy our website as our team members worked hard in learning and understanding coding syntaxes in just a few weeks in order to create this website. Other than that we wish you could relate to our stories as this website's target audience are mainly university students. We love you and we would love you even more if you would help share this website with your friends to help increase our website traffic. With all that said, Have a great time browsing the website!
